Our Journey

From Lahore, With Love

BharkaBeauty began in 2019 as a small boutique in DHA Phase 6, Lahore, founded by Zainab Malik — a beauty enthusiast who was frustrated by the lack of authentic international brands available in Pakistan.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a movement. Within two years, we had served over 10,000 customers and opened our second store in Gulberg. Today, BharkaBeauty is Pakistan's most trusted beauty destination, with a curated online store serving customers nationwide.

Our mission remains the same as day one: to make every Pakistani woman feel beautiful, confident, and celebrated — exactly as she is.

"Beauty has no standard. BharkaBeauty was built to celebrate every shade, every skin type, and every story." — Zainab Malik, Founder & CEO
